Bistro Campagne

French · with Ilyse, Steve, Breanna · Apr 1, 2026
la carte du soir
  • hors d'œuvres
  • Croquettes
  • plats principaux
  • Verlasso Salmon

  • Brioche Bread Pudding

Une belle soirée (a lovely evening) with the parents at this Lincoln Square gem. Bistro Campagne has the kind of warm, candlelit ambiance that makes you want to linger over every course.

The croquettes were the perfect amuse-bouche to kick things off — crispy outside, molten inside, très bien. The Verlasso salmon came beautifully plated, the kind of cuisine soignée (carefully prepared cooking) that makes you slow down and actually taste your food.

Dessert was the real pièce de résistance: the Pot de Crème au Chocolat — silky dark chocolate custard — and the brioche bread pudding with figs and caramel. Both phenomenal. On s'est régalés. We feasted.
